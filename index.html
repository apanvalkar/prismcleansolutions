Turnover Checklist

Standard checklist used for short-stay turnovers (customisable per property).

General Cleaning
  • Dust and wipe all surfaces, ledges, and furniture
  • Vacuum and mop all floors (use eco-friendly products)
  • Empty all bins and replace liners
  • Clean mirrors, windows, and glass doors (streak-free finish)
  • Check and adjust decor for neat alignment
Bedrooms
  • Strip and replace all bedding (freshly laundered)
  • Fluff pillows and smooth duvet covers
  • Wipe bedside tables and lamps
  • Ensure wardrobes and drawers are tidy
  • Check under beds for items left behind
Living Area
  • Dust and polish all surfaces
  • Vacuum couches and cushions
  • Fold throws neatly
  • Wipe TV screens and remotes
  • Straighten furniture and decor
Kitchen
  • Wash and put away dishes
  • Wipe benchtops, stove, oven, and microwave
  • Empty and clean bins
  • Refill essentials (coffee, tea, sugar, salt, etc.)
  • Check fridge for leftovers, wipe shelves
Bathroom
  • Scrub shower, bath, sink, and toilet
  • Polish mirrors and chrome fixtures
  • Replace towels and bath mats
  • Restock toiletries (soap, shampoo, toilet paper)
  • Ensure pleasant, clean scent
Finishing Touches
  • Air out the property (open windows briefly)
  • Place eco-scent diffuser or air freshener
  • Set room temperature (if applicable)
  • Check all lights and appliances are off/on as required
  • Leave calling card on kitchen counter (optional)
